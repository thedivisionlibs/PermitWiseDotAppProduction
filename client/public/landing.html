FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PermitWise.

What is PermitWise?
PermitWise is a permit management platform designed specifically for food vendors. We help you track all your permits, get automatic renewal reminders, and know exactly which permits you need for each city you operate in.
How does the free trial work?
You get 14 days of full Pro plan access, no credit card required. After the trial, you can choose a plan that fits your needs or continue with limited free features.
Which cities do you cover?
We currently have permit data for 50+ major cities across the US, including Austin, Los Angeles, New York, Houston, San Francisco, and more. We're adding new cities every week. Even if your city isn't fully covered, you can still use our tracking and reminder features.
Do you file permits for me?
We don't file permits on your behalf, but we make the process much easier. We tell you exactly which permits you need, provide renewal checklists, auto-fill application forms (Pro plan), and remind you when it's time to renew.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll have access until the end of your billing period.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption for all data. Your documents are stored securely in the cloud with automatic backups. We never share your information with third parties.
