2. Description of Service

PermitWise is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform that provides tools to help mobile vendors, food truck operators, and event organizers manage permits, track compliance, and organize their businesses. The Service includes:

  • Permit Tracking & Management: Track permits across multiple jurisdictions, monitor expiration dates, and receive automated renewal reminders
  • Document Vault: Securely upload and store permits, insurance certificates, inspection reports, and other business documents
  • Compliance Checklists: Access and customize inspection and compliance checklists
  • Permit Requirement Data: Browse city-specific permit requirements for various vendor types
  • Event Management: Create, manage, and track events; handle vendor applications; and monitor vendor compliance (for Event Organizer accounts)
  • Event Attendance Tracking: Track events you plan to attend and monitor your permit readiness
  • Team Accounts: Invite team members with role-based access (available on eligible plans)
  • Notifications: Email and optional SMS reminders for permit renewals, expirations, and event deadlines
