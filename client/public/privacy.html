3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Railway, AWS), email delivery (SendGrid), SMS delivery (Twilio), and payment processing (Stripe). These providers are contractually bound to use your data only to provide services to us.
  • Event Organizers: If you apply to or are accepted to an event through PermitWise, limited business and compliance information (business name, permit status for required permits) may be shared with the event organizer. You initiate this sharing by applying to their event.
  • Team Members: If you add team members to your business account, they may access shared business data, permits, and documents in accordance with their assigned role (owner, manager, or staff).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of PermitWise, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for their own marketing purposes.

4. Document Storage & Security

Documents you upload to PermitWise (permits, insurance certificates, inspection reports, etc.) are:

  • Encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Stored securely in cloud infrastructure with access controls
  • Accessible only by you and authorized team members on your account
  • Deletable by you at any time through the Service

We implement industry-standard security measures including password hashing (bcrypt), authenticated API endpoints, role-based access control, and secure session management. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account remains active and as needed to provide the Service.
  • Closed Accounts: Upon account deletion, we permanently remove your personal data, business data, permits, documents, and associated records from our active systems. Some data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being permanently purged.
  • Legal Holds: We may retain certain information as required by applicable law, regulation, or legal proceedings.
  • Anonymized Data: We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you for analytics and service improvement purposes.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you

  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and data (see Section 7 below)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@permitwise.app or use the in-app tools described in Section 7.

7. Account Deletion

You have the right to permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion:

  • All personal information, business data, permits, documents, inspections, events, and compliance records are permanently removed
  • Any active subscription is automatically cancelled (no further charges will be incurred)
  • Team members on your account will lose access
  • This action is irreversible — data cannot be recovered after deletion

How to delete your account:

  • On the Web App: Go to Settings → Billing, scroll to the "Danger Zone" section, and click "Delete Account Permanently". You will be asked to confirm twice.
  • On the Mobile App (iOS/Android): Go to Settings → Legal & Support, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account Permanently". You will be asked to confirm twice.
  • Via Email: Send a deletion request to privacy@permitwise.app from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

  • Push Notifications: Optional and controlled via your device settings and in-app preferences.
  • Marketing Communications: We may occasionally send product updates or feature announcements. You can opt out at any time via the unsubscribe link or your notification preferences.
